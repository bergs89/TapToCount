Privacy Policy

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use the Tap to Count App application.

Information Collection and Use

The Tap to Count App does not collect any personal data or usage data.

The Tap to Count App operates entirely locally on your Mac. It does not collect, transmit, or store any personal or usage data externally. All counting data created or managed by the app remains exclusively on your device and is never shared or transmitted over the network.

No data processed or created by the Tap to Count App leaves your device. Tap to Count App stores data locally in memory and not on disk.
